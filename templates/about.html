About Reading Challenge

A journey through classic literature together

Our Reading Adventure

Welcome to our shared reading challenge! This is where Silas and Nadine track their progress through a carefully curated collection of 103 classic literature books. From timeless novels to groundbreaking works that shaped literature, we're exploring the greatest stories ever written.

Whether it's diving into Victorian romance, exploring existential philosophy, or discovering forgotten gems, every book read brings us closer to understanding the rich tapestry of human storytelling.

Features

  • Track reading progress for both readers
  • Beautiful book covers from Open Library
  • Rate and review completed books
  • Filter by reader and century
  • Progress statistics and percentages
